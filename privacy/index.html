Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

AAA Handyman Services respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit aaahandyman.services, request service, book an appointment, apply for a job, join an optional promotional list, communicate with us, or use our website chat.

By using the website or submitting information, you acknowledge the practices described here. Please do not submit information you do not want us to process.

1Information We Collect

Service and contact information: name, email address, phone number, city or service location, requested service, project description, preferred appointment date and time, messages, and information needed to prepare a quote, schedule work, or complete a service.

Communications and promotions: call, email, text, chat, and form content; records of requests and responses; and your optional choices about seasonal offers, promotions, or a first-service gift certificate.

Careers information: contact details, location, trade, experience, availability, tools and transportation, application answers, employment-related disclosures you choose or are asked to provide, messages, and uploaded resumes or other application files.

Website and device information: IP address, browser and device type, pages viewed, referring page, approximate location derived from IP, interaction and performance data, and similar technical information collected through logs, analytics, anti-spam tools, cookies, or related technologies.

2How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries, prepare estimates, confirm appointments, provide services, order materials, process payments through the methods you select, and support our one-year workmanship guarantee.
  • Send service-related calls, emails, and texts about inquiries, quotes, scheduling, arrival, project updates, and follow-up.
  • Send seasonal offers or promotional messages when you make an optional selection to receive them, and maintain your communication preferences.
  • Review and respond to careers applications, arrange interviews, evaluate qualifications, and maintain recruiting records.
  • Operate and improve the website, chat, forms, security, accessibility, performance, service offerings, and customer experience.
  • Prevent fraud, spam, misuse, and security incidents; comply with law; enforce agreements; and establish or defend legal claims.

3Texts, Calls & Email

If you provide a phone number or email address, we may contact you about your service inquiry, quote, appointment, project, guarantee request, application, or other transaction. Message and data rates may apply.

Promotional messages are optional and are not a condition of purchasing services. You may opt out of marketing texts by replying STOP, opt out of marketing email through the instructions in the message, or contact us. We may still send non-promotional communications needed to respond to you or provide requested services.

4Website Chat & Automated Tools

Messages entered in the website chat, together with the page you are viewing, may be sent to service providers that help generate and deliver automated responses. Chat responses are provided for general information and may be incomplete or inaccurate.

Do not enter Social Security numbers, financial account details, medical information, passwords, or other highly sensitive information in chat. Contact us directly when a request requires private or detailed project information.

5Cookies, Analytics & Embedded Content

We and our service providers may use cookies, logs, tags, or similar technologies to keep the website working, remember limited preferences, prevent spam, measure traffic and performance, and understand how pages are used. You can control cookies through your browser, although blocking some technologies may affect site operation.

The site may use services such as Netlify hosting, forms, analytics or performance monitoring; Google fonts, maps, or anti-spam tools; and links or embeds from Facebook, Yelp, Nextdoor, payment services, or other third parties. Those services may collect information under their own privacy policies when you load or use them.

6How We Share Information

We may share information with hosting, form processing, communications, analytics, anti-spam, scheduling, AI, storage, payment, material, professional, and other vendors that help us operate the website and provide requested services. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect rights or safety, in connection with a business transfer, or with your direction or consent.

We do not sell personal information. We do not share mobile opt-in information or text-message consent with third parties for their independent marketing. Vendors may process that information only as needed to provide communications or related services for us.

7Retention & Security

We retain information for as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, including service records, warranties, recruiting, legal obligations, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, and business operations. Retention periods vary by the type of record and applicable requirements.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the information we handle. No website, transmission, or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

8Your Choices & Requests

You may ask to review, correct, or delete personal information you submitted, or to update communication preferences. We may need to verify your identity and may retain information when required for legal, safety, warranty, employment, transaction, or recordkeeping purposes.

To make a request, email contact@aaahandyman.services or call (248) 385-3432.

9Children's Privacy

The website and services are intended for adults and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child submitted information so we can review and remove it as appropriate.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the website, forms, services, vendors, or legal requirements. The effective date above identifies the current version. Your continued use of the website after an update acknowledges the revised policy.
